I have never officailly taken any drawing or painting before, so they do not look that great. However, since I am quite proud of my work, I'd like to share them with you all. I do believe that anyone can draw and paint, and every single picture painted with a good heart is always beautiful. Some of these paintings are copy cats of the famous pieces, so I'll let you figure out whose masterpieces I tried to imitate.
